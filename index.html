The Mathswaala Story

Transforming Challenges into Confidence.

Established in 2010, Mathswaala was founded with a singular vision: to transform Mathematics from a daunting subject into a language of logic and confidence. Located at Highland Residency, Balkum Dhokali, we have become the go-to destination for parents seeking the best Maths tuition in Thane West for CBSE, ICSE & State Board.

Guided by our founder, Mr. Pravin Nagendra Singh sir (seen here addressing a class), we provide specialized mentorship that spans from 8th to 12th Standard. Mr. Singh helps students achieve success by simplifying complex financial mathematics, statistics, and calculus into intuitive concepts, ensuring a strong foundation for both school success and future professional careers.

Our coaching methodology is specifically tailored for 8th, 9th, and 10th-grade students as well as 11th/12th Commerce. We don't just solve problems; we build the "why" behind every formula. Under Mr. Singh's dedicated guidance, students develop the self-study habits and critical thinking skills required to build exceptional scores and lifelong mathematical fluency.
