The Mathswaala Story

Transforming Challenges into Confidence.

Established in 2010, Mathswaala was founded with a singular vision: to transform Mathematics from a daunting subject into a language of logic and confidence. Located at Highland Residency, Balkum Dhokali, we are the trusted coaching destination for CBSE, ICSE & State Board.

Guided by our founder, Mr. Pravin Nagendra Singh sir, we provide specialized mentorship spanning from 8th to 12th Standard. We help students achieve success by simplifying complex concepts for both Science and Commerce streams, ensuring a strong foundation for future professional careers.
